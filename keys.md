### 不同操作系统下特殊按键对比说明：
|  macOS | Windows
| ---|---
| ⌘ command | win
| ⌥ option | alt

| ⌃ control | ctrl
| ⇪ caps lock | Caps Lock
| ⌅ enter | enter
| ⎋ esc | esc
